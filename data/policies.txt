RETURNS AND REFUNDS POLICY
Eligibility
- Return window: 30 calendar days from delivery date (receipt by you).
- Condition: Unused, in original packaging with all accessories and manuals.
- Non-returnable items: Gift cards, downloadable/digital goods after download/access, perishable items, customized products, and items marked “Final Sale.”
- Proof of purchase required.

Process
1) Request RMA: Contact support with order number and reason for return.
2) Pack: Include RMA in the box; use original packaging when possible.
3) Ship: Use the provided label (if applicable). Return shipping cost:
   - Defective/incorrect item: Paid by us.
   - All other returns: Paid by you.
4) Inspection: 2–3 business days after receipt at our facility.

Refunds
- Method: Issued to the original payment method only.
- Timing: We initiate within 2 business days after inspection; your bank may take 5–10 additional business days to post.
- Amount: Product price minus shipping fees and applicable restocking fees.
- Restocking fee: 10% for opened/used items in resellable condition; waived for defects or incorrect shipments.
- Store credit: Available upon request instead of a refund.
- Exchanges: Subject to stock availability; otherwise processed as refund then new order.

Late or Missing Refunds
- If not received after 12 business days from our confirmation, contact your bank first, then contact support with your RMA and bank reference.
